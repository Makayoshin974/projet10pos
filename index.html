In the spirit of ideas worth sreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together ti share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video live speakerscombine to sparks deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organised TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidancefor the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.
